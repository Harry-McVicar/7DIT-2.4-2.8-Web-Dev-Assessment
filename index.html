Welcome To The Computer Science Hub

Computer science is the study of computers and computer concepts including their systems, design, development and use. In this course you will learn the three essesial computer science concepts of encryption, error control and computer security. The focus of these concepts is to protect private data by preventing hacks and errors from occuring during the completion of everyday tasks. We will begin with an introduction into each of these three concepts before relating them back to their implications in the real world.
